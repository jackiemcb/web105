Angel Stadium

My husband and I decided to take a last minute trip to Angel Stadium in Anaheim. It was within walking distance of our resort, and tickets were only around ten dollars! The stadium is not only home to the Los Angeles Angels, but also a hub of sports and entertainment. Watching a game here was an electrifying experience. The stadium's unique design offers fantastic sightlines from every seat, ensuring a great view of the action. We enjoyed classic stadium treats like hot dogs and peanuts while taking in America's favorite pastime. We also got to check out the Angels Baseball Hall of Fame to learn about the team's history. Whether you're a die-hard fan or a casual observer, Angel Stadium is a hit for everyone
