Amusement

Santa Monica Pier, located in the heart of the city, offers an unforgettable combination of amusement park and seaside escape. With a vintage Ferris wheel, roller coaster, and various carnival games, it's a delightful mix of nostalgia and excitement. The Pacific Park rides provide stunning ocean views. If you're looking for more leisurely activities, the beach, shopping, and street performers offer a diverse range of entertainment. Stroll along the wooden planks, indulge in cotton candy, or enjoy fresh seafood at a nearby restaurant. Santa Monica Pier embodies the California spirit of fun, sun, and sea.
